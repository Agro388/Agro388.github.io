AB-016

“A star burns brightest when someone powerful finally sees it as a star.” — AB-016

Classification & Status

Classification: Anomalous Anthropomorphic Wolf / Stellar Entity

Status: Active / Uncontained / Partially Stabilized

Classified breach log ████-██

Incident: massive spatial distortion, partial collapse of containment facilities, temporal anomalies observed

Observers note reality instability and anomalous phenomena in proximity

Appearance

Detailed appearance is password-protected.

Humanoid Wolf Form

  • Tall, lean, digitigrade anthro wolf build
  • Fur: matte black or dark ash grey with glowing constellation-like fracture lines
  • Eyes mismatched: Left - glowing artificial eye with shifting patterns; Right - void-black, sometimes flashes star-core in Stellar Form
  • Ears: one often notched/torn
  • Tail: long, leaves faint star-trails when moving
  • Branding / marks: “AB-016” etched on upper torso or neck
  • Clothing: torn containment cloak / straps / broken limiter cuffs fused into wrists or ankles
  • Presence: causes minor reality distortion, sound echoes, gravity inconsistencies

Stellar Form (Unnatural State)

  • Fur deepens to void-black, fracture-lines like flickering constellations
  • Halo rings (1–2) orbit, cracked and unstable
  • Wings form from fragmented starlight, partial or glitching
  • Star-core eye flickers; gaze can cause vertigo and temporal disorientation
  • Mental strain is extreme; maintaining identity while in form is taxing

Stellar Form (Partially Accepted)

  • Energy flows more smoothly, fracture-lines stabilize
  • Wings semi-consistent
  • Abilities more reliable, reduced sensory overload
  • Presence awe-inspiring but not chaotic

Stellar Form (Fully Accepted / Mythic Potential)

  • Constellation lines fully aligned and glowing
  • Halo rings smooth, orbit with precision
  • Wings fully formed and ethereal
  • Eye stabilized, reality-resistant, presence legendary
  • Powers operate at intended efficiency

Personality

  • Cold, observant, highly tactical
  • Self-defined morality; not malevolent by default
  • Protective toward weaker or exploited beings
  • Rarely speaks; communication is concise and impactful
  • Rejects ownership, labels, and confinement
  • Stellar Form triggers are reactive to threats against autonomy

Relationships

  • Lost World Scientists: Creators / captors / former enemies
  • Other AB Units: Possible kinship, awareness unclear
  • Fantasy World Inhabitants: Unwilling protector / feared entity
  • Overseer Entities / Archangels / Mythic Gods: Recognition required for full potential
  • AB-014: Mentor / peer influence; can stabilize Nova slightly but cannot fully unlock Stellar Form

Abilities

Detailed abilities are password-protected.

  • Reality rule bypass (localized and unstable)
  • Ability nullification and suppression (partial in unstable Stellar Form)
  • Extreme physical adaptability
  • Superhuman strength, speed, perception
  • Phase movement / short-range teleportation
  • Temporal resistance (partial)
  • Dimensional awareness
  • Predatory cognition (threat prediction)
  • Stellar Howl: wave disrupts powers/tech (nerfed if unstable)
  • Powers evolve mid-conflict to adapt to threats
  • Mental strain and inefficiency occur if Stellar Form is used without recognition

Acceptance Mechanics

Detailed mechanics are password-protected.

  • Recognition from a single mythic entity (archangel, “father,” Lucifer, etc.) enables partial stabilization
  • Full potential requires multiple divine recognitions
  • AB-014 recognition alone provides minor stabilization, insufficient for full Stellar Form
  • Recognition must be voluntary, conscious, and meaningful
  • Acceptance affects Stellar Form stability, ability efficiency, and reality interaction

Weaknesses

  • Form is unnatural, burns energy excessively
  • Mental strain in Stellar Form if rejected
  • Partial recognition limits abilities
  • Vulnerable to long-term attrition, emotional manipulation, conceptual threats
  • Cannot fully ignore external forces until fully accepted

Containment

  • Currently impossible
  • Attempts at direct force result in:
    • Stellar Form destabilization
    • Reality collapse
    • Temporal anomalies
  • Observers recommend:
    • Avoid confrontation
    • Attempt recognition-based negotiation for partial stabilization
    • Engagement only if containment is absolutely necessary

Trivia

  • Nova refuses names except self-designation
  • Acts as both predator and reluctant guardian
  • Presence in unstable Stellar Form alters local reality
  • Only stabilizes with acknowledgment from higher powers
  • AB-016 represents existence beyond ownership

Snapper

Description

Snapper is a sentient machine powered by millions of vengeful souls that were killed by the Oculus Caelorum, contained in a crystal which also serves as its power source. Since it doesn’t have the power to hunt down Oculus Caelorum directly, its sole goal is to eliminate individuals who support or allied with the Oculus Caelorum. Its origin is unknown; it appears randomly, and the exact computational resources it uses remain mysterious.

Abilities

Detailed abilities are password-protected.

  • Main weapon: scavenged fire axe
  • Extreme speed, precision, and agility
  • Superhuman strength
  • Punches and kicks for difficult opponents
  • Highly resistant to bullets, fire, and completely waterproof
  • Thermal, infrared, and ultraviolet vision with nearly 360° coverage (cameras on shoulders/backhand)

Rending Apart

Due to high resistance to most elements, only high-caliber firearms can weaken Snapper, but won’t fully eliminate it. Complete destruction requires removing all armor bolts and attacking its internal components. Even waterproof, it sinks rapidly in deep waters.

Relationship Details

United States government

Classified Snapper as a highly dangerous terrorist. Snapper targets political figures associated with the Oculus Caelorum.

Oculus Caelorum

Snapper’s consciousness is formed from souls killed by the Oculus Caelorum, making it vengeful.

Alexander Clarissimus

Tracked, fought, and survived Snapper multiple times. Snapper respects Alexander with fear after being burnt black.

Trivia

  • The crystal powering Snapper is part of an Oculus Caelorum organ
  • Snapper avoids brass objects despite having brass horns
  • Owns a pair of circular glasses with one cyan and one orange lens; rarely worn

Appearance

Alex is an average-looking white man, 6’1” tall, 23 years old.

In Vulcan Knight form: full body plated armor made of copper, brass, and bronze, with a fire symbol on the chest. Gas mask under the helmet with two lenses (cyan and orange). Two batons attached to forearms, backpack with two tanks, tube connecting to a modified M2 flamethrower attached to the left arm.

Personality

Alex is a parallel to Snapper; he believes every life should not be exchanged for the greater good. He dedicates his life to stopping Snapper by any means, and possesses extreme determination.

Abilities

Detailed abilities are password-protected.

  • High durability against sharp and blunt attacks in Vulcan Knight form
  • Batons act as shields and melee weapons
  • Main weapon: flamethrower fueled by napalm (operable with one hand)
  • Gas mask protects against poison, smoke, ash, dust, and sand
  • Exceptional combat skill, tracking, and ambushing Snapper

Rending Apart

Weaknesses include range and speed. Face is unprotected when helmet visor is up. Flamethrower can run out of fuel and requires significant time to refill.

Relationship Details

Benjamin Pochurek

Friend and ally; provides materials, engineering, and assistance to create the Vulcan Knight suit. Often acts as Alex’s getaway driver.

Snapper

Primary target. Alex constantly tracks Snapper’s location and ambushes it as the Vulcan Knight.

Trivia

Character Description

AB-016, known as Nova, is an anthropomorphic wolf-like stellar anomaly whose power does not naturally stabilize on its own. Unlike traditional god-tier or anomaly entities, Nova’s full potential is conditional, dependent on metaphysical recognition by higher cosmic authorities such as archangels or mythic gods.

Without recognition, Nova’s advanced forms manifest as unnatural and incomplete, causing instability and internal strain. While Nova possesses the framework of a deity-class entity, he is not inherently acknowledged as one—placing him in a liminal state between anomaly, protector, and unrealized god.

Appearance

Base Form: An anthropomorphic wolf with subtle star-like markings in the fur. Physically imposing but mostly grounded, with minimal anomalous emissions.

Stellar Form (Unnatural / Unaccepted): Bright stellar fractures across the body, unstable halo structures, and partially formed wings composed of light and void. Visual intensity is high, but symmetry and control are lacking.

Stellar Form (Accepted): Refined cosmic patterns, stable halo geometry, fully realized wings resembling constellations. Light emission becomes controlled and deliberate rather than chaotic.

Note: If affected by AB-014’s permanent ability disable, all forms persist visually but are non-functional.

Personality

Nova is reserved, observant, and introspective. He does not seek domination, worship, or authority—only acknowledgment of existence. Rejection manifests as withdrawal and instability rather than aggression.

Abilities

Normal State (No Ability Disable)

  • Superhuman strength, speed, and perception
  • Interdimensional travel
  • Stellar energy manipulation
  • Limited reality interaction
  • Gradual anomalous adaptability

Form-Based Scaling

  • Base Form: Minimal anomaly access
  • Unnatural Stellar Form: High output, unstable control
  • Partially Accepted: Controlled, reliable abilities
  • Fully Accepted: Maximum potential, perfect control

Under AB-014 Permanent Ability Disable

All anomalous abilities are permanently disabled:

  • Interdimensional travel
  • Reality or time manipulation
  • Energy projection
  • Regeneration and adaptability
  • Dimensional or mental abilities

Only physical traits remain:

  • Enhanced strength
  • Reflexes
  • Durability
  • Cosmetic form changes

Rending Apart

There is no confirmed method of bypassing AB-014’s permanent ability-disabling power. Acceptance, recognition, form evolution, or divine acknowledgment cannot restore disabled abilities once removed.

Relationship Details

  • Lost World Scientists: responsible for creation and initial containment; contributed to instability.

  • AB-014: stabilizing influence and mentor-like peer; recognition alone cannot unlock full Stellar Form.

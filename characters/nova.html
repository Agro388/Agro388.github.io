Overview

Nova is an anthropomorphic wolf-like stellar anomaly whose power does not naturally stabilize on its own. Unlike traditional god-tier or anomaly entities, Nova’s full potential is conditional, dependent on metaphysical recognition by higher cosmic authorities such as archangels or mythic gods.

Without recognition, Nova's advanced forms manifest as unnatural and incomplete, causing instability and internal strain. While Nova possesses the framework of a deity-class entity, he is not inherently acknowledged as one, placing him in a liminal state between anomaly, protector, and unrealized god.

Appearance

Base Form

An anthropomorphic wolf with subtle star-like markings in the fur. Physically imposing but mostly grounded, with minimal anomalous emissions.

Incomplete Stellar Form

Bright stellar fractures across the body, unstable halo structures, and partially formed wings composed of light and void. Visual intensity is high, but symmetry and control are lacking.

Complete Stellar Form

Refined cosmic patterns, stable halo geometry, fully realized wings resembling constellations. Light emission becomes controlled and deliberate rather than chaotic.

Personality

Nova is reserved, observant, and introspective. He does not seek domination, worship, or authority, only acknowledgment of existence. Rejection manifests as withdrawal and instability rather than aggression. Despite possessing god-adjacent potential, Nova consistently demonstrates empathy toward mortals and lower entities.

Abilities & Scaling

  • Physical: Enhanced strength, reflexes, durability, and superhuman speed.
  • Cosmic: Interdimensional travel, stellar energy manipulation, and gradual anomalous adaptability.

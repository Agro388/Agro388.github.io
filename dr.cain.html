Overview

Amber Cain is one of the first human hybrid experiments created by Genesis Biolabs. She is the founder and current CEO of The Prehistoric Sanctuary. Despite her advanced age of 46, she maintains a youthful appearance due to a reverse aging process. She holds significant global influence, possessing control over the Bohemian Grove that exceeds even that of the U.S. President.

Appearance

Physiology

5 foot 6 inches tall with pale ivory skin. Due to her hybrid nature, she possesses lime green eyes with vertical slit pupils (Bamboo Viper) and maroon-tipped teeth with sharp canines (Short-tailed Shrew).

Style

She typically wears a casual hoodie from The Prehistoric Sanctuary. Her hair is styled with short bangs and a short, pointed ponytail. She is occasionally seen with heterochromic orange and cyan glasses.

Personality

Amber initially projects a cheerful, friendly, and professional demeanor. However, she harbors traits of sociopathy, sadism, and narcissism. While generally "nice" by default, she is prone to manic speech and can become exceptionally brutal toward those who repeatedly annoy or aggravate her.

Abilities & Scaling

Genetically engineered strength, thermal vision, venomous saliva (inducing paralysis), expert marksmanship with high-caliber revolvers, reverse aging, hyper-metabolism, and elite private security coordination.

Rending Apart

Despite her genetic enhancements, she remains fundamentally human. Her primary weakness is range; she relies on a medium-ranged revolver and bodyguards. In combat, keeping distance is the most effective strategy to avoid her venomous bite and security detail.

Relationship Details

Genesis Biolabs: The company that created her as a major project, providing the initial resources for her sanctuary.

Elon Musk: A client and friend. Amber assists him with Moon/Mars colonies and sold him her de-aging treatment.

Martin Starlit: A close friend and "Edener" who trusts her deeply, even when he disapproves of her darker impulses.

Charlotte Splendere: A childhood friend who is protective and excitable. Amber provided her with the same de-aging treatment.

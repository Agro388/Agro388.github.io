Overview

Ab-014 is a nearly omnipotent being, made by an unknown civilization to test extremely rapid biological evolution, and later escaped. In a young and inexperienced state, they accidentally created the Oculus Caelorum species. They are the primary author of these wiki records and exist at a level of power that allows them to manipulate reality, time, and the stabilization of other anomalous entities.

Appearance

Current Form

A young androgynous humanoid with a light grey skin tone and shoulder-length jet black hair with long bangs. Their body is completely greyscale. Their face is most often not visible, either covered by hair, blurred, censored, scrambled, or just blank. They wear an opened dark grey jacket with a white shirt underneath (partially showing the word “freedom”) with nondescript black pants and shoes.

Anomalous Features

They possess a halo and rarely large wings, all in colors of cyan, white, and orange that glow faintly. They are sometimes seen wearing circular glasses with heterochromic lenses (one orange, one cyan).

Evolutionary History

2nd: Amoeba-like organism.
3rd: Eyeless Brown Marmorated Stink Bug (0.5 inches).
4th: Faceless replica of a lead scientist.
5th: Human-sized lava creature (brown, red, orange, and yellow).

Personality

Ab-014 is apathetic, quiet, and antisocial, very careless about anything and everything. They often do things that would hurt others or themselves for entertainment. They describe themselves as "kinda stupid and lazy."
